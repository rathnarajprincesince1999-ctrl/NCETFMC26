About

About SSN

Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering (SSN), an autonomous institution affiliated to Anna University, founded by Dr. Shiv Nadar, Chairman Emeritus & Strategic Advisor to the Board – HCL Tech, philanthropic venture to give back to the society that nurtured him.

SSN is accredited by NAAC with an A++ grade and ranked 47th in the Engineering Category and 82nd among all educational institutions in India Rankings 2025 by NIRF, MHRD.

About the Department

The Department of Chemistry serves to provide strong foundation in Applied Chemistry essential for all Engineering streams. A separate Chemical Science Research Centre has been established to carry out research in socially relevant technologies.

The department has been recognized as a Research Centre by Anna University, Chennai for conducting Ph.D. program.

About NCETFMC'26

National Conference on Emerging Trends in Functional Materials Chemistry– NCETFMC'26 aims to bring national researchers, academicians and industrialists under one roof to discuss various advancements in the field of functional materials for future applications.

The conference will consist of Keynote, invited talks, oral and poster paper presentations.

Conference Topics

Porous polymers
Polymeric materials, Soft Materials & Biomaterials
Materials for Energy Storage and conversion
Nanoporous and Nanostructured materials
Upcycling/recycling of waste polymers

Theory, Modeling & Simulation
Thin Films, Surfaces & Interfaces

Environmentally friendly material
